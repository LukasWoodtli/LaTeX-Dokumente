\section{Trigonometrie}
\label{sec:Trigonometrie}

h: Hypotenuse\\
a: Ankathete\\
g: Gegenkathete
\[
sin(\alpha)=\frac{g}{h}
\]
\[
cos(\alpha)=\frac{a}{h}
\]
\[
tan(\alpha)=\frac{g}{a}
\]
\[
tan(\alpha)=\frac{sin(\alpha)}{cos(\alpha)}
\]
\[
sin^2(\alpha)+cos^2(\alpha)=1
\]
